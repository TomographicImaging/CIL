        :param gm_domain: domain of the operator 
        :param gm_range: range of the operator, default: same as domain
        
        
        Note: 
        
        .. math::

                O^{*}: Y^{*} -> X^{*} \text{(Adjoint)}
        
                < O(x), y > = < x, O^{*}(y) >
                       
     '''
